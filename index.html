About Us

CSI Immanuel Church is a Christ-centered, Bible-based, and Spirit-filled congregation rooted in the rich heritage of the Church of South India (CSI). For decades, our church has stood as a beacon of faith, hope, and love in our community—faithfully serving God and people. At CSI Immanuel, we believe that the Church is not just a building, but a living, worshiping, and serving family. Our sanctuary is a place of reverent worship, passionate praise, and heartfelt prayer. From Sunday services and Bible teachings to outreach programs and community care, everything we do is centered on glorifying Christ. We are committed to nurturing spiritual growth for all generations—children, youth, adults, and elders. Our church empowers every believer to live out their calling, equipped with the Word of God and filled with the Holy Spirit. Whether you are seeking a deeper relationship with Christ or a community to belong to, CSI Immanuel Church welcomes you with open arms. Join us as we grow together in faith, serve with love, and shine as the light of Christ in the world.
